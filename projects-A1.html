    Ancient China

    This project originated from a roleplay and collaborative storytelling community with friends and other developers who participated in their spare time. In this community, I played the role of a Han dynasty-esque empire which required me to model assets and write entire story arcs, pushing my skills in writing and modelling to the absolute limit.

    Military Aircraft

    This project started mostly as a friendly competition between a group of developers for a flightsim. Each developer would try to outdo the other in terms of how "cool" of a jet they could make to be imported and added to the game. Though I always lost to those who exclusively used blender, I gained many skills in CSG modelling and visual design.
